Programming is the process of creating instructions that tell a computer how to perform a specific task or set of tasks. These instructions, known as code, are written in various programming languages, each with its own syntax and rules. The code is then interpreted or compiled by the computer to execute the desired functionality.

Programming is highly significant in today's world for several reasons:

1. **Automation**: Programming allows us to automate repetitive and time-consuming tasks, increasing efficiency and productivity across industries.
2. **Problem Solving**: Programmers can solve complex problems and streamline processes in various fields, such as healthcare, finance, and engineering with software applications and algorithms.
3. **Innovation:** Programming is a driving force behind technological advancements, enabling the development of new tools, platforms, and systems that transform the way we live and work.
4. **Economic impact**: The software industry plays a pivotal role in the global economy, significantly contributing to revenue generation and creating numerous employment opportunities across various sectors.
5. **Accessibility**: With the proliferation of smartphones and the internet, programming has made information, services, and resources more accessible to people worldwide.
6. **Education**: Learning to code has become an essential skill, promoting logical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and creativity, which are valuable in many aspects of life.

In summary, programming is a fundamental tool that shapes our modern world, driving innovation, efficiency, and progress across various sectors. As technology continues to advance, the importance of programming will only continue to grow.

### What Will We Cover?

In this first lesson, we will explore the history of programming to understand how it has evolved and continues to shape the future.

We will start by with the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and some common models that guide our approach to programming tasks and development needs. Next, we will explore programming algorithms, which are step-by-step instructions used to solve specific problems with code. Algorithms are fundamental to the SDLC; they are conceptualized, selected, implemented, tested, and refined. The quality and correctness of algorithms directly impact the functionality and performance of software, forming the foundation of all programming.

After covering some basic programming concepts, you will learn where and how to write Python programming code within an [Integrated Development Environment (IDE)](#transformedTip0tooltip) IDE may just seem like a fancy programming term, but it is an editor for writing and running code. .

Finally, we will introduce key Python commands and concepts. Both the SDLC and IDEs are essential for Python and programming concepts as they provide structured methodologies and comprehensive tools for developing, testing, and maintaining efficient and scalable software applications.

### Examples of Programming Languages

Programming languages serve as the primary medium for creating software solutions, enabling developers to implement algorithms, manage data, and interface with system components effectively; through this capability, programming has fundamentally transformed how the world operates, how businesses function, and how complex problems are solved.

It touches every industry today, from logistics and banking to scientific research. Whether it's the apps on your smartphone or advanced medical technologies that save lives, programming directly influences and enhances every aspect of our daily lives.

Here are a few examples of how companies from different industries are solving business problems using programming:

#### ![medical professional holding a cellphone and typing on it](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1391/files/620119/download)

#### Flatiron Health

**Healthcare Business Problem**

Healthcare data is often siloed and unstructured, making it difficult for healthcare providers to access and analyze patient information effectively.

**Solution**

Flatiron Health developed a cloud-based platform using programming languages like Python and Java to collect, structure, and analyze real-world oncology data. This platform enables healthcare providers to make more informed decisions, improve patient outcomes, and accelerate research in cancer treatments.

#### ![partial view of a laptop with a cellphone showing stock performance next to it](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1391/files/620127/download)

#### Stripe

**Finance Business Problem**

Online businesses often face challenges when integrating payment processing into their websites or applications, dealing with various payment methods, and ensuring secure transactions.

**Solution**

Stripe, a financial technology company, built a developer-friendly payment processing platform using programming languages such as Ruby, Python, and JavaScript. Their APIs and SDKs allow businesses to easily integrate payment functionality into their applications, handle various payment methods, and ensure secure transactions, thus simplifying the payment process for online businesses.

##### ![cellphone with UPS logo on it](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1391/files/620279/download)

#### UPS

**Logistics Business Problem**

Managing a global logistics network involves complex challenges, such as optimizing routes, tracking packages, and ensuring timely deliveries.

**Solution**

UPS heavily relies on programming to develop sophisticated algorithms and systems that optimize their global logistics network. They use programming languages like C++, Python, and Java to create applications for route optimization, package tracking, and automated sorting in their facilities. These systems help UPS streamline operations, reduce costs, and improve the overall efficiency of their logistics processes.

These examples demonstrate how programming can be applied in diverse industries—such as healthcare, finance, and logistics—to solve specific business problems. By leveraging the power of programming, these companies have developed innovative solutions that improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance the quality of their services.
