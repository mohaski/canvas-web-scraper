### STEP 2: The SBI Feedback Model

**Giving constructive feedback:** Use SBI - Situation, Behavior, Impact

| Step | What to Say | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Situation** | When and where | "In yesterday's stand-up..." |
| **Behavior** | Observable action | "...when you interrupted me..." |
| **Impact** | The effect | "...I lost my train of thought." |
