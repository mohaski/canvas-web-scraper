## Professional Development for Technical Professionals

**Time Required:** 2 hours over the next week  
**What You'll Do:** Watch videos, reflect, and connect with peers

---

### Why This Matters

You're here to build technical skills in **Data Science**. Those skills will get you in the door.

But technical skills alone won't advance your career. Employers consistently report: *"Great technical skills, weak communication and collaboration."*

This module helps you develop the **professional skills** that complement your technical training. These aren't just "soft skills" - they're essential skills for:

* Explaining technical work to non-technical stakeholders
* Collaborating effectively with teams
* Managing projects and priorities
* Building your professional reputation

---

### What's in This Module?

**Session 1: Professional Identity** (30 min)  
Discover what success looks like in your technical field and reflect on your professional strengths.

**Session 2: Communication Essentials** (45 min)  
Learn frameworks for clear, effective communication in technical roles.

**Session 3: Collaboration & Ethics** (30 min)  
Understand how to work effectively with others and navigate ethical situations.

---

### How This Works

✅ **Watch** short videos   
✅ **Reflect** on how this applies to you  
✅ **Connect** with peers in your cohort for practice and application

---

**⏱ Time Commitment:** About 2 hours total, at your own pace this week.

**🎯 Goal:** Get oriented to professional development and start thinking about your career identity.
