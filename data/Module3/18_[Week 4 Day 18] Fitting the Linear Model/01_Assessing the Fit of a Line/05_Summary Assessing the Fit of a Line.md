### Terms Recap

* **Coefficient of Determination (
  ![r squared](https://learning.flatironschool.com/equation\_images/r%255E2?scale=1)
  r2):** Measures the proportion of variance in the dependent variable explained by the independent variable(s).
* **Residuals:** The differences between observed and predicted values in the regression model.
* **Residual Plot:** A scatterplot used to check for randomness in residuals, helping identify assumption violations.
* **Influential Values:** Points that disproportionately affect the regression model's slope or intercept.
* **Outliers:** Data points that deviate significantly from the model's predictions.

### Key Concepts

* **Evaluating Model Fit:** Use **![r squared](https://learning.flatironschool.com/equation\_images/r%255E2?scale=1)
  r2** to determine how well the model explains the variability in the data.
* **Analyzing Residuals:** Residual plots and diagnostics like Cook's Distance help identify influential points and outliers.
* **Interpreting Results:** Ensure the model aligns with the observed data by addressing anomalies or patterns in residuals.

### Brief Process Overview

1. Verify Data Suitability
2. Define the Variables
3. Scatterplot
4. Compute the Regression Line and *r*
5. Compute **![r squared](https://learning.flatironschool.com/equation\_images/r%255E2?scale=1)
   r2**
6. Analyze Residuals
7. Identify Influential Observations
8. Refine the Model
9. Interpret Results
