### Terms Recap

* **Linearity:** Relationship between *x* and *y* must be linear.
* **Independence of Errors:** Residuals should show no correlation with the independent variable.
* **Normality of Errors:** Residuals should follow a normal distribution.
* **Equal Variances:** Residuals must have constant variance across all values of *x*.

### Key Concepts

Ensuring linear regression assumptions are met allows the model to provide reliable predictions and statistical inferences.

**Diagnostics** such as scatterplots, residuals vs. fits plots, histograms, and Q-Q plots are essential tools for validating these assumptions.

### Brief Process Overview

1. Verify Linearity
2. Check Independence of Errors
3. Verify Normality of Errors
4. Check Equal Variances
5. Address Violations
