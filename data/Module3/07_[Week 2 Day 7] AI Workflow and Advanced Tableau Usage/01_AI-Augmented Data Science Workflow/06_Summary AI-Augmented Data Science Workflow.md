### Terms Recap

* **Data Science Workflow:** Systematic sequence for data analysis and modeling.
* **Generative AI:** Systems creating content from learned patterns.
* **Augmented Intelligence:** Human-AI collaboration for enhanced capabilities.
* **Adaptive Automation:** Context-aware process streamlining.

### Key Concepts

* Integration of AI tools throughout CRISP-DM workflow.
* Balance between automation and human oversight.
* Systematic assessment and implementation process.
* Quality control and validation frameworks.
* Continuous feedback and optimization loops.

### Brief Process Overview

1. **Assessment:** Identify workflow bottlenecks and opportunities.
2. **Design:** Map data flows and define success criteria.
3. **Implementation:** Begin with pilot projects, build libraries.
4. **Integration:** Choose appropriate human-AI collaboration model.
5. **Quality Control:** Monitor, validate, and optimize performance.
