Every day, businesses face decisions that could significantly impact their bottom line: Should we implement this new feature? Will this design change increase conversions? Is our new marketing campaign actually performing better than the old one? While intuition and experience play important roles, making decisions based on gut feelings alone can be risky and costly. This is where A/B testing transforms gut feelings into measurable, testable propositions.

**Digital experimentation transforms the theoretical concepts of statistical testing into practical business applications.** While statistical testing provides the mathematical framework, digital experimentation adds layers of technical, user experience, and business considerations that make A/B testing a unique discipline.

It represents the intersection of three key domains: statistical methodology, software engineering, and business strategy. While traditional statistical testing provides tools to analyze data, digital experimentation adds layers of complexity that make it a distinct discipline.
