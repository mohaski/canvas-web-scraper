1. Create a Tableau Account

Before we get into defining what Tableau is and how it works, you'll want to download Tableau Public (free version of Tableau) and set up an account.

Go to the [Tableau website


Links to an external site.](https://public.tableau.com/s/). Click the Sign Up for Tableau Public button.

[![Tableau Public home screen with Sign Up button inside red rectangle](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1426/files/631524/download)](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1426/files/631524/download "Click on the Sign Up for Tableau Public button to create a free account.")


Click on the Sign Up for Tableau Public button to create a free account.

1. Download Tableau Public

Once you have created and signed into your account, click the button: [Download Tableau Public.


Links to an external site.](https://www.tableau.com/products/public/download)

![Tableau Public download screen with the Download button highlighted.](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1426/files/631527/download)

Click on the Download Tableau button to begin your download.

1. **Follow the prompts**

Follow prompts on the next screen to download and install Tableau Public on your computer.

1. R**estart your computer**

Restart your computer and launch the Tableau Public application from your desktop.
