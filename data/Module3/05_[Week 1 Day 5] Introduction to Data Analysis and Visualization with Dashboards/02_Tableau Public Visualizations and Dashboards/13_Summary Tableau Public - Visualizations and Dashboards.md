### Terms Recap

* **Dashboard:** A collection of visualizations on a single page.
* **Worksheets:** Individual visualizations.
* **Layout:** The arrangement of elements on a dashboard.
* **Filters:** Interactive elements allowing users to modify data views.
* **Containers:** Groups of related sheets on a dashboard.

### Key Concepts

* **Data Source:** A data source is a bridge between Tableau and the underlying source data.
* **Measures and Dimensions:** Two essential concepts that help users to create interactive and informative data visualizations.
* **Interactive Dashboards:** Adding interactivity to a dashboard is a great tool to let your viewer adjust the view of the data to meet their needs.

### Brief Process Overview

1. Plan your dashboard.
2. Create individual visualizations.
3. Create a new dashboard.
4. Arrange and customize the layout.
5. Add sheets.
6. Arrange and resize sheets.
7. Add filters.
8. Add titles and text.
9. Refine appearance.
10. Publish and share.
