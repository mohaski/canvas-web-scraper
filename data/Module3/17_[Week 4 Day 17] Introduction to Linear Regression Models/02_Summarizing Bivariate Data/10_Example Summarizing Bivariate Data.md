Imagine you're analyzing the relationship between daily app usage time (*x*) and monthly active users (*y*):

* **Scatterplot:** Visualize the data to identify a possible trend.
* **Regression Line:** Use the regression equation to model and predict *y* based on *x*.
* **Correlation Coefficient (*r*):** Quantify the relationship to validate the strength of the observed trend.

### Industry Standard Comparisons

1. **E-commerce:** Assess the relationship between ad spend and sales revenue.  
   * A strong positive *r* indicates ad spend significantly impacts revenue.
   * The regression line predicts future revenue based on budgets.
2. **Healthcare:** Analyze the effect of reminder frequency on appointment attendance.  
   * A regression model helps determine the optimal number of reminders for improving attendance rates.
3. **Transportation:** Evaluate the relationship between ride availability and customer satisfaction scores.  
   * Correlation analysis identifies key variables to focus on for service improvements.
