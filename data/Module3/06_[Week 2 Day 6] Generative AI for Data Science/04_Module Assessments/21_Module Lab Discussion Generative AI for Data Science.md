# Module Lab Discussion: Generative AI for Data Science

## ![White FS Logo](https://moringa.instructure.com/courses/1426/files/631557/preview) Lab Discussion: Generative AI for Data Science

This is the discussion for the module lab. This discussion is optional. For each of the three scenarios, share the prompts you used and the code generated in the the scenario discussion. As a part of this discussion, explain why you set up your prompt how you chose and any potential hurdles you may have encountered.

### Scenario 1

* How did different prompts handle date calculations?
* What visualization approaches were suggested?
* How was error handling implemented?

### Scenario 2

* How did different prompts approach error identification?
* What validation methods were suggested?
* How was time handling improved?

### Scenario 3

* How were percentile calculations handled?
* What approaches to date filtering were used?
* How was the query optimized, CTE, subquery etc…?

### Instructions: Response to Peer

1. After posting your initial response and reviewing your peers' submissions, select one classmate's post, and choose one of the following areas to discuss:

* + Prompt 1, 2 or 3 : what were the differences or similarities within the prompts?
  + How could you develop your prompting to get similar responses or differences?

1. Identify the prompt you are discussing in your response.
2. Be sure to provide detailed suggestions or questions about the prompt included in your response.

### Submission and Grading Criteria

1. Click reply below to create a new discussion post. You will post for each scenario separately (for a total of 3 discussion posts). Be sure to:

* Answer all three questions for each scenario.
* Include the prompt you used for each scenario.
* Discuss any challenges or hurdles you experienced with the lab.
* Provide an interaction post to a classmate.
