### 1. Problem Definition & Input Preparation

* Define specific task requirements.
* Gather necessary data or context.
* Structure your initial prompt.
* Consider desired output format.
* Identify constraints or limitations.

### 2. Initial Generation & Review

* Submit well-structured prompt.
* Generate initial output.
* Review for accuracy and relevance.
* Identify gaps or areas needing improvement.
* Check for any potential errors.

### 3. Refinement & Iteration

* Adjust prompts based on initial results.
* Add specific details or constraints.
* Generate revised outputs.
* Fine-tune based on feedback.
* Maintain version control of changes.

### 4. Validation & Testing

* Verify accuracy of generated content.
* Test functionality (if code).
* Compare against requirements.
* Check for edge cases.
* Ensure compliance with standards.

### 5. Implementation & Documentation

* Integrate validated output.
* Document process and decisions.
* Note any modifications made.
* Record limitations or concerns.
* Create usage guidelines.

### Example: Analyzing Customer Data

For example, if analyzing customer data, you would:

1. Define the analysis needs and gather data.
2. Generate initial analysis code.
3. Refine the analysis based on results.
4. Validate findings against known data.
5. Document insights and process.

This process ensures systematic, reliable use of Generative AI while maintaining quality control and proper documentation.
