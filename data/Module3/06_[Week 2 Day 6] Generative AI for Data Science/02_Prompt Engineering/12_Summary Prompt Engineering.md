### Terms Recap

* **Prompt Engineering:** Art of crafting effective AI instructions.
* **Context:** Background information for AI tasks.
* **Constraints:** Specific limitations and requirements.
* **Input Formation:** Structure of initial requirements.
* **Output Specification:** Expected format and standards.
* **Iteration:** Process of refining prompts.
* **Role-Based Prompting:** Assigning specific expertise to AI.

### Key Concepts

* **Clarity and Specificity**
  + Clear task definition
  + Detailed requirements
  + Explicit constraints
  + Expected outcomes
* **Technical Context**
  + Programming environment
  + Tools and frameworks
  + Performance requirements
  + Quality standards
* **Iterative Improvement**
  + Testing responses
  + Refining prompts
  + Optimizing outputs
  + Documentation

### Brief Process Overview

1. Problem Definition & Scope
2. Context Setting & Framework
3. Initial Prompt Construction
4. Refinement & Testing
5. Iteration & Optimization
