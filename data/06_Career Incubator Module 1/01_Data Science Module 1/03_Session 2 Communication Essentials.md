### STEP 2: The Audience Awareness Framework

Before communicating anything technical, ask these 5 questions:

| Question | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| **1. Who is my audience?** | Technical peers? Manager? Executives? Clients? |
| **2. What do they already know?** | Adjust technical depth accordingly |
| **3. What do they NEED to know?** | Focus on what matters to them |
| **4. What decision are they making?** | Help them decide or take action |
| **5. What action do I want?** | Be explicit about next steps |

### STEP 4: Practice Exercise (15 min)

**Choose one scenario :**

**🔵 Data Science - Scenario 1:**

Explain what "correlation vs. causation" means to someone with no statistics background. Use a real-world example (ice cream sales and drownings, etc.). 3-4 sentences, no math terms.

**🟢 Data Science - Scenario 2:**

Take a recent data analysis from your work and write a 1-paragraph executive summary for your CEO. Focus on business decisions and impact, not methodology. What should they DO with this information?
