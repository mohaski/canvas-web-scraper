As you have already seen in this lesson, for loops are a powerful tool in Python, allowing you to automate repetitive tasks on sequences of elements. They provide a concise and efficient way to iterate through lists, tuples, strings, dictionaries (for keys), and sets. By understanding iterative processes and how to use them with for loops, you can effectively process and manipulate data structures in your Python programs. As you progress in your Python journey, for loops will become an essential building block for more complex and powerful programs.

### Terms Recap

* **For loop:** A programming construct for iterating over a sequence.
* **Iterative:** An object that can be looped through one element at a time.
* **Loop variable:** A variable that takes on the value of each element in the sequence during each iteration.

#### Key Concepts

For loops:

* automate repetitive tasks on sequences.
* provide a concise way to iterate through elements.
