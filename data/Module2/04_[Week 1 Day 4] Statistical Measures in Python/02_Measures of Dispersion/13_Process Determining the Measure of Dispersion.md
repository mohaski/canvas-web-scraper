1. Determine the shape of the distribution. Depending on the case you may already know the shape or you may have to determine it using a data visualization.
2. Assess which of the three statistical measures yields a characteristic value for the data.  
   1. “Bell-shaped” use standard deviation
   2. “Skew” use IQR
   3. MAD is good in particular for cases with extreme values
3. Calculate the appropriate statistic for your data in Python.  
   1. Having Python give you both the standard deviation as well as the IQR is good practice.
