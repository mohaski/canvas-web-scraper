In this topic, you will be introduced to the all important endeavor of merging, reorganizing, and aggregating data by group. You will learn key Pandas functionality for each of these tasks.

Data science projects often involve **merging/joining** tabular data from disparate sources*—*each dataset containing a facet of the information required to tackle a given problem. After cleaning the merged data, you will then go about exploring your data.

**Reshaping** your data into different formats and calculating summary statistics across different categories can enable you to draw comparisons between various factors and groups in a more effective manner. This is key in exploratory data analysis: where you are trying to discover what aspects of your data are important in addressing the overarching problem. In addition, reshaping and grouping will be an essential step in any final analysis and preparation of data for any subsequent modeling steps.
