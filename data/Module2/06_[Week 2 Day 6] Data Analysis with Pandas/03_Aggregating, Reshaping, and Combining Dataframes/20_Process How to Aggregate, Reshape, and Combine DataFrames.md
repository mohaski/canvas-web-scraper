The steps to aggregate, reshape, and combine DataFrames includes the following:

1. Load multiple datasets required for a data analysis.
2. Clean datasets individually as necessary.
3. Join datasets and clean as required.
4. Reshape dataset as convenient for various tasks associated with data exploration and analysis.
5. Groupby and aggregate for drawing statistical comparisons across groups*—*both during exploratory data analysis and during final analysis addressing initial problem.
