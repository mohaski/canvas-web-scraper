# **Module Overview**

Data does not speak for itself. Behind every insight is a human who must decide what it means, who needs to hear it, and how to make it land. Module 2 is about building that bridge from raw analytical output to decisions that matter.

In this module you will practice two interlocking skills: Communicating for Impact and Project Management. These are not soft add-ons to your technical toolkit they are what transform a correct analysis into a useful one.

 **Why This Matters**

Studies consistently show that data analysts who can communicate findings clearly earn 20–30% more than those who cannot. More importantly, analyses that are never understood are never acted upon, they simply do not count.

Project Management is equally non-negotiable. Without structured planning, even brilliant analytical work arrives too late, goes over scope, or fails to meet stakeholder expectations.

**Module Learning Objectives**

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

* Translate complex data insights into clear, compelling narratives for non-technical audiences
* Select and create appropriate visualizations that enhance understanding and drive decision-making
* Write executive summaries that distill analytical findings into actionable recommendations
* Plan and execute data analysis projects with clear milestones, timelines, and deliverables
