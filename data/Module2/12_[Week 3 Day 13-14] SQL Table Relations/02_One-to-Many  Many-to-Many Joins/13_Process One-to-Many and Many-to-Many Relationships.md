The process for building One-to-Many and Many-to-Many Relationships includes the following steps:

1. Identify the columns and which tables you need.
2. SELECT the columns and tables.
3. Create the proper kind of JOIN that you need (inner, outer, left, right &c).
4. Filter the data as needed.
5. Sort your final results.
6. Refine the query depending on the output.
